About Royal Palace

Established in 1920, the Royal Palace Hotel has been the preferred choice for discerning travelers seeking an unparalleled luxury experience. Our majestic property blends historic grandeur with contemporary comforts.

Nestled in the heart of the city, our hotel offers breathtaking views, exquisite dining, and impeccable service that has delighted royalty, celebrities, and world leaders for over a century.

Every detail at Royal Palace has been carefully curated to provide our guests with an unforgettable experience of opulence and refinement.
